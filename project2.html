Spring 2022

This project, "Adventure RPG Fantasy," was developed using GML and JavaScript. The game features a rich storyline, diverse characters, and engaging quests. Players can explore various landscapes, battle enemies, and complete missions to progress through the game.

The game incorporates advanced AI for enemy behaviors, a dynamic weather system, and a comprehensive inventory system. The characters and environments are designed with detailed pixel art to create an immersive experience.

The game was left unfinished and was made as a personal project interest to learn more about game design.
